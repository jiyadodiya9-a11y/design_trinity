🌍 About Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only planet known to support life. It is the largest of the four rocky planets in our solar system. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water, which is why it appears blue from space.

Earth has a protective magnetic field generated by its liquid iron core. This field shields us from harmful solar radiation. Earth also has a thick atmosphere made mostly of nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%), which keeps the planet warm and supports life.

Earth is home to over 8 million species of plants and animals. It has one natural satellite — the Moon — which stabilizes Earth's tilt and causes ocean tides. Earth formed about 4.5 billion years ago from a cloud of gas and dust.
